 <p style="text-align: right;">龚道国诗歌</p> 
 <p>◎桃 树</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly"></p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">陶公这一走，将一双归去来兮的</p> 
<!-- more --><p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">青色布鞋，复行数十步</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">悄悄印在了此地。又长出了桃树</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">一脚穿上它，根子穿深了</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">穿上它，让一种站立向上下用力</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">向下去的，一脚踩进了土</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">扎向深处，坚持着隐蔽和挖掘</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">向上去的，克服了天气和情绪</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">克服了对于高度的欲望</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">用一种休闲的姿势，旁生侧长</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">用一种蓬勃的轻松，拥抱了天空</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">穿上它，一棵桃树用一生的情趣</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">穿出陶公的影子。花去果熟</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">香散甜聚。一棵桃树终其一生</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">在内心里安居，在枝叶间轻移</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">◎桃 花</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">桃花有它非凡的坚决，在它面前</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">任何犹豫都是卑微的。一朵</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">桃花的绽放，像一个热恋的人</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">打开自己的心脏，不留瞬间的</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">迟疑，一眼望到心底</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">一朵桃花，有它深爱的季节</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">和天空。任何羞涩都是羞愧的</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">在爱的时光里，红是心脏</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">奔放的血液。一杯血液的火焰</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">比酒更醉，醉到毁灭，乱飞</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">这是爱的一生，烂漫而短暂</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">彻底而决绝。岁月排着漫长的队列</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">花排着花，停顿在那儿</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">◎桃花飞扬</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">风只是稍加提醒，就知道花到艳时</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">不该再迷恋高处了</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">飞向大地去，在清风中一瓣一瓣地</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">告别自己，记下每一瓣美丽</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">让自由亲爱过自己</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">飞到溪里。原来幸福是水做的歌啊</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">幸福等待着，一群欣闻的舞者</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">一群听歌的耳朵</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">◎桃花流水</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">青色的溪水，叫上比溪水更青色的</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">春天，叫上那些桃花，春天的眼睛们</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">一路游转，在青天下淌过青山去</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">叫上桃花的影子。牵着另一群影子</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">虫鸟和云片。叫上忙碌的人，在岁月的</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">长廊里，停顿和奔跑，仿若桃花</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">◎美好的桃子</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">在甜蜜和苦涩之间，隔着一层坚韧</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">甜蜜是红色的，也是水做的</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">我们尝它往往浮于表面，浅尝即止</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">而苦难总是落到深处</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">苦通常是白色的，也有些微黄</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">但是美好的桃子，让世界充满甜蜜</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">让更多的人度过蜜月的颜色和味道</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">而核仁坚持了苦涩的内心</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">深居且沉静，有一种无言的坚韧</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">就在这里，定然有一个人</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">从站立到弯曲，起身时完成了种植</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">照心的形状种下果核。种下果核的</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">坚不可摧。它的上面</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">烙满历程，它的里面隐居着心灵</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">◎桃骨子</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">我对朋友说，别让一枚桃骨子刺着你的小舌子。朋友来不及住口</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">牙缝里尖锐地挤兑了一声</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">上下两排牙梆，像慌忙的钉书机</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">卡在我小小的劝告上</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">现在他满口都是红的。桃子的红与血液的红，用甜咸两种相反的味道</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">模糊他喜欢狡辩的小舌子</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">而桃骨子陶瓷一样的尖角</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">也从他看上去很美的牙齿间</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">找到了缝隙。在长句子的习惯上</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">插进一个不太习惯的标点</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">朋友说，想不到舌头还不够柔软牙齿还不够结实。这时</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">夺匡而逃的桃骨子滚落在大地上</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">展开一片血性方刚的图腾</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">也亮出一身阡陌，坎坷纵横</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">◎桃 林</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">我站在这里，代替一个人</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">穿过紫陌红尘，且走过前世今生</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">站在一千树桃花面前</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">代替一个人独行，饮酒，写诗</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">踽踽之中，写下桃树裂开的一个个</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">伤口，写下风在伤口上的一次次</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">吟咏，写下伤口错过春天的叹息</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">写下伤口开合不定的变迁</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">桃树上已经没有怒放的火焰</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">只有星星和闪耀的温暖</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">&nbsp;</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">代替一个人居住，用一间陋室</p> 
 <p style="line-height:19.0pt;mso-line-height-rule:exactly">住进百亩桃林，也用岁月散落的</p> 
 <p>满地碎片，开出暖暖的花瓣&nbsp;</p>
